Some of us, in our professional relation, at one point get this delusion, that without us, without us working, everything would fall appart.

And if we invest a lot of ourselves, if failure happens, it also means we fall appart. Metaphorically speaking.

Vicious cycle of burnout:

If we don't give our everthing, we don't maximize chance of success. Then we increase a chance of failure. Failure means we lose our investment. So we have to invest even more, in this case ourselves.
